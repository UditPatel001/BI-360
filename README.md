# Business Insights 360 – Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview
Business Insights 360 is an end-to-end **Business Intelligence dashboard built using Power BI** to analyze organizational performance across **Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive functions**.  

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, analyze trends, and support **data-driven decision-making**.

## Key Business Metrics

- **Net Sales:** $3.74B  
- **Gross Margin:** 38.08%  
- **Gross Margin Value:** $1.42B  
- **Net Profit Margin:** -13.98%  
- **Forecast Accuracy:** 81.17%

---

## Dashboard Views & Insights

### Finance View
Provides **Profit & Loss analysis**, highlighting **$3.74B Net Sales** and **$1.42B Gross Margin**, with **-13.98% Net Profit Margin**, indicating profitability challenges driven by high operational costs.

### Sales View
Analyzes **customer performance and revenue contribution**.  
For example, **Amazon generated ~$496.88M in Net Sales**, making it one of the top revenue-generating customers.

### Marketing View
Evaluates **product segment performance**, where **Notebooks lead with $1.58B in sales**, followed by **Peripherals ($897.54M)** and **Desktop ($711.08M)**.

### Supply Chain View
Tracks operational metrics such as **Forecast Accuracy (81.17%)**, **Net Error (-3.47M)**, and **Absolute Error (6899K)** to identify **Out-of-Stock and Excess Inventory risks**.

### Executive View
Provides a **high-level strategic overview** of business performance across markets.  
For example, **North America contributes $1.02B in revenue**, while **India generates ~$945M**, highlighting key revenue-driving regions.
